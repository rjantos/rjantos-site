Carrington Law Firm is proud to announce that several of its attorneys have been featured in the _National Law Review's_ annual spotlight on legal excellence. This recognition highlights the firm's commitment to providing top-tier legal services and its ongoing leadership in the legal community.

### Highlights

- Attorneys featured for expertise and dedication
- Annual spotlight on legal excellence
- Ongoing leadership in the legal community

### Attorney Profiles

Meet some of the featured attorneys:

- **Emily Chen** – Renowned for her work in intellectual property law, Emily has successfully defended clients in high-stakes patent disputes and regularly speaks at national conferences.
- **James Patel** – A leader in corporate law, James is known for his strategic counsel in mergers and acquisitions, helping clients navigate complex regulatory environments.
- **Maria Gomez** – With a focus on family law, Maria has advocated for vulnerable clients and contributed to policy reforms in child custody cases.

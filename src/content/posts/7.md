## Program Overview

Carrington Law Firm has launched a new internship program designed to mentor aspiring attorneys and provide hands-on experience in various legal fields. The inaugural class includes 12 interns from top law schools across the country.

### Program Features

- Training in litigation, corporate law, and family law
- Networking events with partners and alumni
- Professional development opportunities, including resume workshops and mock interviews
- For law students and recent graduates
- Community service projects and pro bono work

### Intern Experiences

- Interns shadowed attorneys in court and participated in client meetings
- Group project: Drafted a legal memorandum for a real case
- Attended a seminar on ethics and professional responsibility

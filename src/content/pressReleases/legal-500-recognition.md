# Carrington Law Recognized as Top Litigation Firm by Legal 500

**New York, NY – November 28, 2024** – Carrington Law has been named a top litigation firm in the 2024 Legal 500 rankings, highlighting the firm's exceptional performance in complex litigation matters and commitment to client success.

## Recognition Details

- **Litigation Excellence**: Ranked among the top 10 litigation firms in the United States
- **Client Satisfaction**: Praised for innovative legal strategies and dedicated client service
- **Practice Areas**: Recognized for expertise in commercial litigation, employment law, and intellectual property disputes

"This recognition reflects our team's unwavering commitment to achieving the best possible outcomes for our clients," stated Litigation Partner Sarah Mitchell. "We are honored to be recognized by Legal 500 for our dedication to excellence in litigation."

The firm's litigation practice serves clients across various industries, providing strategic legal representation in high-stakes disputes and complex legal matters.

For more information about Carrington Law's litigation practice, visit [carringtonlaw.com/practice-areas/litigation](https://carringtonlaw.com/practice-areas/litigation).

**About Carrington Law**  
Carrington Law is a full-service law firm specializing in corporate, litigation, and business law. With offices in New York, Los Angeles, and Chicago, the firm serves clients nationwide.

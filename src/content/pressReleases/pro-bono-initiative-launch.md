**New York, NY – October 20, 2024** – Carrington Law today announced the launch of its comprehensive pro bono initiative, "Justice for All," aimed at providing legal services to underserved communities and promoting access to justice across the United States.

## Initiative Overview

- **Expanded Pro Bono Services**: Increased commitment to providing free legal services to low-income individuals and families
- **Community Partnerships**: Collaboration with local organizations to identify and address legal needs
- **Specialized Programs**: Focus on housing law, family law, and immigration matters for underserved populations

"Access to justice is a fundamental right that should be available to everyone, regardless of their economic circumstances," said Managing Partner Jonathan Sullivan. "Through this initiative, we are expanding our commitment to serving our communities and making a meaningful impact on access to justice."

The Justice for All initiative will provide legal representation in critical areas including housing disputes, family law matters, immigration proceedings, and consumer protection cases.

For more information about Carrington Law's pro bono programs, visit [carringtonlaw.com/community](https://carringtonlaw.com/community).

**About Carrington Law**  
Carrington Law is a full-service law firm specializing in corporate, litigation, and business law. With offices in New York, Los Angeles, and Chicago, the firm serves clients nationwide.

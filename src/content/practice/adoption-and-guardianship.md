We assist clients in navigating adoption and guardianship processes, helping to secure loving, legally recognized relationships for children and families.

## Adoption Services

- Domestic adoption guidance
- International adoption support
- Step-parent and relative adoptions
- Foster care adoption
- Adoption home studies and assessments
- Post-adoption legal services

## Guardianship Services

- Adult guardianship proceedings
- Minor guardianship arrangements
- Conservatorship and power of attorney
- Special needs guardianship
- Guardianship modifications and terminations

## Legal Documentation

- Adoption decrees and finalization
- Birth certificate amendments
- Immigration and citizenship support
- Inheritance and estate planning

Our team provides comprehensive support throughout the adoption and guardianship journey, ensuring all legal requirements are met and families are protected.

We guide families through custody decisions with a focus on creating stable, supportive arrangements that prioritize the best interests of the children involved.

## Custody Services

- Child custody evaluations and assessments
- Parenting plan development
- Visitation and access arrangements
- Relocation and move-away cases
- Modification of existing custody orders
- Enforcement of custody agreements

## Child Support

- Child support calculations and agreements
- Modification of support orders
- Enforcement of support payments
- Medical and educational expense allocation

## Parental Rights and Responsibilities

- Termination of parental rights
- Adoption and guardianship matters
- Grandparent visitation rights
- Step-parent adoption

Our attorneys approach custody matters with sensitivity and expertise, advocating for arrangements that promote children's well-being and family stability.

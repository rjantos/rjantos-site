# Carrington Law Recognized as Top Litigation Firm by Legal 500

**New York, NY – September 28, 2024** – Carrington Law has been recognized as a top litigation firm in the 2024 Legal 500 rankings, receiving top honors for its business litigation and dispute resolution services.

## Prestigious Recognition

The Legal 500's 2024 edition highlights Carrington Law's exceptional performance in several key areas:

- **Business Litigation**: Ranked in Band 1 for comprehensive dispute resolution services
- **Commercial Disputes**: Recognized for expertise in complex commercial litigation
- **Trial Practice**: Noted for successful courtroom representation and strategic advocacy

## Client-Focused Approach

"Our recognition in the Legal 500 reflects our unwavering commitment to achieving the best possible outcomes for our clients," said Litigation Partner Maria Gonzalez. "We combine legal expertise with strategic thinking to resolve disputes efficiently and effectively."

## Industry Leadership

The Legal 500 rankings are based on extensive research and client feedback, making this recognition a testament to Carrington Law's position as a leading litigation firm. The firm's attorneys have successfully handled complex cases across various industries, including technology, finance, healthcare, and manufacturing.

## Continued Excellence

This recognition builds on Carrington Law's tradition of excellence in litigation. The firm continues to invest in its litigation practice, ensuring clients receive the highest quality representation in all legal matters.

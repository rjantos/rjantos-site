# Carrington Law Opens New Los Angeles Office

**Los Angeles, CA – July 5, 2024** – Carrington Law announced today the opening of its new Los Angeles office, expanding the firm's West Coast presence and bringing comprehensive legal services to clients in the entertainment, technology, and creative industries.

## Strategic Location

The new office, located in the heart of Beverly Hills, positions Carrington Law to serve the unique legal needs of California's entertainment and technology sectors. The 15,000-square-foot facility includes state-of-the-art conference rooms, client lounges, and dedicated spaces for creative industry consultations.

## Specialized Practice Areas

The Los Angeles office will focus on practice areas critical to West Coast clients:

- **Entertainment Law**: Music, film, television, and digital media
- **Technology Law**: Software, apps, AI, and emerging technologies
- **Intellectual Property**: Copyright, trademark, and patent protection
- **Real Estate**: Commercial and residential transactions
- **Employment Law**: Entertainment industry contracts and disputes

## Experienced Leadership

The office will be led by Senior Partner David Chen, a recognized expert in entertainment law with over 20 years of experience representing major studios, production companies, and creative professionals. Chen will be joined by a team of experienced attorneys specializing in various aspects of entertainment and technology law.

## Commitment to Local Community

"Carrington Law is excited to bring our tradition of excellence to the West Coast," said Chen. "Los Angeles is a hub of creativity and innovation, and we're committed to supporting the legal needs of this dynamic community."

## Seamless Integration

The Los Angeles office will operate seamlessly with Carrington Law's New York headquarters, providing clients with consistent, high-quality legal services across both coasts. The firm will offer video conferencing capabilities and cross-office collaboration to ensure comprehensive service delivery.

# Carrington Law Expands Corporate Practice with New Partner Acquisition

**New York, NY – October 15, 2024** – Carrington Law is pleased to announce the addition of three senior partners to its corporate practice group, significantly enhancing the firm's capabilities in mergers and acquisitions, corporate governance, and complex business transactions.

## New Partners Join the Team

The new partners bring over 75 years of combined experience in corporate law:

- **Sarah Chen**, formerly a senior partner at a leading international law firm, specializes in cross-border mergers and acquisitions with a focus on technology and healthcare sectors.
- **Michael Rodriguez**, recognized as a leading expert in corporate governance and securities law, has advised Fortune 500 companies on compliance and regulatory matters.
- **Jennifer Walsh**, a veteran litigator in complex commercial disputes, strengthens the firm's trial capabilities in corporate litigation.

## Strategic Expansion

"This strategic expansion positions Carrington Law as the premier destination for sophisticated corporate legal services," said Managing Partner Jonathan Sullivan. "Our clients will benefit from enhanced expertise across the full spectrum of corporate law matters, from formation and governance to complex transactions and dispute resolution."

## Commitment to Excellence

The additions reflect Carrington Law's ongoing commitment to providing exceptional legal services and maintaining its position as one of New York's most respected law firms. The firm continues to invest in talent and resources to serve its growing client base effectively.

## About Carrington Law

Carrington Law is a full-service law firm with offices in New York and Los Angeles, providing comprehensive legal services to businesses and individuals. The firm specializes in corporate law, litigation, real estate, employment law, intellectual property, and estate planning.
